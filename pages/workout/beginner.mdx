# Beginner workout

<Important>
  If the other program is too hard from the beginning I found a beginners
  workout that you can do from the start. I suggest you do this the first 4
  weeks and then switch over to the other one I made:
</Important>

EVERY DAY: On waking up

- Drink one big cup of black coffee
- One pressed lemon with plenty of water, add in something for taste (not sugar).

<Important>
  Suggestion: 10g glutamin, 5g eaa and 5g bcaa. Then 20 minutes of fast walking.
</Important>

## Full Body Workouts for Beginners:

### Beginners Full Body Workout A:

- [Barbell back squats](https://youtu.be/i7J5h7BJ07g), 3 sets of 8 – 12
- [Back pulldown](https://youtu.be/MyQVLFBwlJ8?t=330) (use assistance if necessary – machine or bands) – 3 sets of 8 – 12
- [Overhead Press](https://youtu.be/oBGeXxnigsQ) (seated or standing, dumbbell or barbell), 3 sets of 8 – 12
- [Dumbbell single arm row](https://youtu.be/pYcpY20QaE8?t=27), 3 sets of 8 – 12
- [Bench press](https://youtu.be/YQ2s_Y7g5Qk) (dumbbell or barbell), 3 sets of 8 – 12

### Beginners Full Body Workout B:

- [Romanian deadlift](https://youtu.be/JCXUYuzwNrM?t=177), 3 sets of 8 – 12
- [Barbell bent over row](https://youtu.be/6FZHJGzMFEc), 3 sets of 8 – 12
- [Dumbbell walking lunges](https://youtu.be/vni4lElTvsY?t=20) (or barbell reverse lunges), 3 sets of 8 – 12
- [Machine Lat pulldown](https://youtu.be/vjrEYyQ6EW0?t=11), 3 sets of 8 – 12
- [Incline bench press](https://youtu.be/TrTSvn5-MTk) (dumbbell or barbell), 3 sets of 8 – 12

**Day 1: A**  
**Day 2: Rest**  
**Day 3:B**  
**Day 4: Rest**  
**Repeat**
